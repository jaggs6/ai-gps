# Apply to Real Work

This is the step where mastery installs. Skip it and you'll know GPS in theory but reach for AI the same lazy way you always have.

## Your task

Pick a real decision or task you're sitting on right now. Not a hypothetical. Something where the answer matters and you've been putting off thinking hard about it.

Examples (you should pick yours, not these):
- A hire/fire/promotion call
- Pricing change
- Whether to launch, kill, or pivot a project
- A difficult conversation you need to plan
- A technical architecture decision
- A career move (job change, raise ask, side project to bet on)
- An investment thesis you're testing
- A product strategy choice

The decision should be: real, yours, and currently unresolved.

## How the coaching works

Tell the AI what the decision is in 1-3 sentences. The AI will then walk you through running the full GPS sequence on it. Specifically:

1. **Initial prompt** — most people start too vague. The AI should push you to be specific about your context.
2. **Gaslight** — you write it yourself. The AI critiques. Most people are too gentle on the first try.
3. **Push back** — you write the push back after seeing the AI's response. The AI critiques.
4. **Stress test** — you run all three moves. The AI points out what you'd otherwise miss.
5. **Evaluate** — is the final answer something you'd actually act on, or is it just smart-sounding? If smart-sounding, you keep going.

## What "done" looks like

Done = you have an answer you'd commit to in writing. Not a balanced list. Not "it depends." A concrete next step you can take this week.

If you can't get there in 6-8 turns, the issue is usually one of:
- **Not enough context** — gap check should catch this. Provide more upfront.
- **Too easy on the AI** — push harder. The third or fourth push back is where the real answer lives.
- **The decision isn't actually a decision** — it's a feeling. In which case, that's the real thing to figure out first.

## After

Once you've done this once on something that matters, run it again next week on something else. The reflex builds with reps. Three real applications and GPS becomes how you talk to AI by default.
